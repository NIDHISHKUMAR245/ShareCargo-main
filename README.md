## 🚢 ShipShare
AI-Powered Container Sharing & Logistics Optimization Platform
---

## 📖 Overview
ShipShare is a web-based logistics platform designed to optimize maritime transportation by enabling multiple users to share container space. The system addresses inefficiencies in traditional shipping by allowing users to book partial container capacity, thereby reducing costs and maximizing resource utilization. It integrates intelligent shipment matching, transparent pricing mechanisms, and real-time tracking to deliver a streamlined logistics experience.
